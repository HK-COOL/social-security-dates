# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Social Security Dates is an independent payment date checker. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Social Security Administration.

## Information we do not collect

The payment date checker does not ask for or require your Social Security number, full date of birth, bank account number, routing number, address, phone number, email address, Medicare number, my Social Security login, or exact benefit amount.

## Information you choose in the checker

The checker uses only non-sensitive choices such as benefit type, birthday range, and selected month. These inputs are used to estimate a scheduled payment date in your browser.

## Official accounts and payments

This site does not enroll direct deposit, update Direct Express, manage benefits, verify identity, or contact Social Security for you. For account, payment method, or benefit-record changes, use official SSA.gov or GoDirect.gov channels.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this site, email hello@socialsecuritydates.com.
