# Terms of Service

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Social Security Dates provides independent, informational estimates of scheduled Social Security and SSI payment dates using public SSA payment rules and calendars. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Social Security Administration.

## No official benefit or payment action

This site is not a government website, benefit portal, payment processor, bank, Direct Express service, or Social Security representative. It cannot confirm that a payment has been issued, posted, delayed, replaced, approved, denied, or changed.

## Date estimates

Payment dates shown by the checker are scheduled-date estimates. They are not guarantees that money has been issued by SSA or posted by a bank or Direct Express account.

## Sensitive information

Do not enter Social Security numbers, bank information, routing numbers, full dates of birth, account logins, or other sensitive personal information into this site. The checker does not need them.

## Official resources

For benefit, identity, payment method, missing payment, or account questions, use official SSA.gov resources or contact Social Security directly.
